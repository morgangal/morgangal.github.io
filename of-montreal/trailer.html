Documentary Trailer

In 2014, a documentary titled, The Past is a Grotesque Animal after their iconic twelve minute song, was released about the prolific band, of Montreal. The film details the evolution of the band, from the very beginning, through their peak, to the falling out. Featured are various band members that have come and gone throughout the years, friends of the band, and associates who toured alongside the band at some point in their career. It focuses mainly on Kevin Barnes and his personal relationships with his band members, love interests, and eventual family.
